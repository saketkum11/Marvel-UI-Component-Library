Rating

Introduction

Usually seen in E-commerce websites and in e-ticket booking systems, ratings act as an indicator of how good a product / movie / show or per say any form of art, product or service is.

Use the class filled to indicate a full star and class half-filled to indicate half filled star, and non of these to indicate empty star. ratings-count is optional to use.
