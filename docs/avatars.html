Avatars

Introduction

Avatars give each user their own identity on the ever growing number of social platforms. Using our various types of avatars, you can give your users flexibililty in the way they want to express themselves, while also ensuring the avatars stick to your theme. Each avatar must have the class avatar inside it's parent. There are four sizes available: avatar-sm , avatar-md , avatar-lg & avatar-xl
